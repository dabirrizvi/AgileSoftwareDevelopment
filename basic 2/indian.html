Indian scrambled masala egg

Serves

2

Prep Time

20 Mins

cooking instructions

step 1

Chop the onions and tomato in small pieces.

step 2

Slice the green chilli into 4 halves.

step 3

Whisk the eggs with 1 teaspoon of salt , 1/2 teaspoon of pepper ,1/2 teaspoon of red chilli powder, 1/4 teaspoon of garam masala and add the green chilli into the egg mixture.

step 4

Add 2 teaspoon of butter on a frying pan on a medium heat.

step 5

Fry the chopped onions for 3-4 minutes until it turns light brown in colour then add chopped tomato and stir for a minute.

step 6

Add the egg mixture to the frying pan and stir constantly to get the eggs scrambled.

step 7

Occasionally stir for 3 minutes until the egg is cooked

step 8

Add coriander leaves for garnish

step 9

Serve either on its own or with some bread toast.

ingredients

3 eggs

1 chopped onion

2 chopped tomato

1 green chilli

1/2 teaspoon of red chilli powder/ paprika

1/4 teaspoon of garam masala

1/2 teaspoon pepper (adjust to taste)

1 teaspoon of salt (adjust to taste)

2 teaspoon of butter

1 gram of coriander leaves (optional- for garnish)
